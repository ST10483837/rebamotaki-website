About Our Company

Building better businesses through compliance excellence. We help Construction, ICT, and Transport companies meet regulatory requirements with confidence.

Who We Are

RebaMotaki Consultants is a specialized consulting firm offering legal and regulatory compliance solutions to businesses in the Construction, ICT, and Transport sectors. In today's dynamic regulatory environment, staying compliant isn't just about avoiding penalties—it's about building trust, mitigating risk, and achieving long-term operational success.

We help our clients do exactly that. With a team of seasoned legal experts, compliance consultants, and industry advisors, we deliver actionable, sector-specific guidance that ensures your operations meet local, national, and international regulatory standards.

We currently operate from our head office in Pretoria with a dedicated team of over 6 professionals. We proudly serve approximately 20 clients operating across diverse industries throughout all 9 provinces in South Africa.

Leadership & Expertise

Mudau Hudivha

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

"Compliance is not just about rules. It's about building better businesses and creating real opportunities for growth and success."

Hudivha has over five years of experience helping businesses meet legal and compliance requirements, especially in BBBEE, ISO standards, and Skills Development. He has worked with many companies in the Construction, ICT, and Transport sectors, helping them follow the law, improve their business processes, and grow in a way that is fair and sustainable.

He is passionate about making compliance simple and useful. Hudivha believes that following the rules should not just be about avoiding penalties—it should help businesses become stronger and more successful.
