B-BBEE Compliance Services

We simplify and support your B-BBEE journey with practical, results-driven solutions to improve your Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment score and enhance your business competitiveness.

B-BBEE Scorecard Elements

Ownership (25 points)

Black ownership and economic interest in the enterprise.

Weight: 25%

Management Control (19 points)

Black representation in senior management and board positions.

Weight: 19%

Skills Development (25 points)

Investment in development of black employees' skills.

Weight: 25%

Enterprise & Supplier Development (44 points)

Support for black-owned suppliers and enterprise development.

Weight: 44%

Socio-Economic Development (5 points)

Contributions to socio-economic development initiatives.

Weight: 5%

Our B-BBEE Service Offering

1 Assess

We review your current B-BBEE status, identify gaps, and highlight opportunities for improvement through comprehensive analysis.

  • Current score assessment
  • Gap analysis
  • Opportunity identification

2 Plan

We develop a clear, tailored B-BBEE strategy to help you achieve your desired score and compliance goals with realistic timelines.

  • Customized B-BBEE strategy
  • Implementation roadmap
  • Priority action plan

3 Implement

We guide and support you in executing key initiatives such as skills development, employment equity, supplier development, and ownership solutions.

  • Skills development programs
  • Supplier development support
  • Ownership structure guidance

4 Prepare

We assist with all verification documentation, file preparation, and compliance evidence to ensure a smooth audit process.

  • Documentation preparation
  • Audit file compilation
  • Evidence gathering support

5 Support

We provide ongoing year-round advice, monitoring, and updates to keep you compliant and progressing toward your B-BBEE goals.

  • Ongoing compliance monitoring
  • Regular progress reviews
  • Legislative update alerts

Benefits of Improved B-BBEE Score

Increased Business Opportunities

Access to government and corporate tenders that require specific B-BBEE levels.

Enhanced Competitiveness

Better positioning in the market compared to competitors with lower B-BBEE scores.

Improved Stakeholder Relations

Enhanced reputation with customers, suppliers, and the community.

Tax Incentives & Benefits

Potential tax benefits and incentives for improved B-BBEE performance.

Improve Your B-BBEE Score Today

Contact us for a free B-BBEE assessment and discover how we can help you achieve a better score.
